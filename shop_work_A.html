Fox Dream

Fox Dream is a silent visual tale of a white fox chasing a red-eyed rabbit in a snowy forest. Mistaking red things for its eyes, it dives into illusions. Red marks grow. Immersed in longing, it rises— only to face the rabbit again. A quiet story of how desire changes us, and how some obsessions remain even after release. The Golden Pinwheel Young Illustrators Competition Finalist, 2025 Bologna Children’s Book Fair Finalist, 2026
